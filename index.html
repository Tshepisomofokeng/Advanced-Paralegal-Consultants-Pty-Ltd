About Advanced Paralegal Consultants (Pty) Ltd

Advanced Paralegal Consultants (Pty) Ltd is a professional paralegal services firm dedicated to supporting law firms and members of the public across South Africa. Founded by Mr Mfanelo Advance Chauke, a certified and trained paralegal, the firm was established with a clear mission to make legal services more accessible, affordable, and easier to navigate.


We provide reliable legal drafting, document preparation, and administrative support services that simplify legal processes while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, confidentiality, and integrity.

Our Professional Paralegal Services

Advanced Paralegal Consultants (Pty) Ltd provides reliable and affordable paralegal services tailored to both law firms and members of the public across South Africa. Our services are designed to simplify legal processes while ensuring accuracy, efficiency, and confidentiality.

Paralegal Support for Law Firms

We provide professional administrative and legal support services to assist law firms with drafting, case management, court processes, and document handling.

  • Legal drafting and research
  • Case and client administration
  • Document preparation (indexing & pagination)
  • Issuing and filing of court documents
  • Sheriff and service attendance
  • Court Online & CaseLines management

Paralegal Services for Members of the Public

We assist individuals with affordable legal document preparation and guidance within the scope of paralegal practice in South Africa.

  • Uncontested divorce assistance
  • Will drafting and estate planning
  • Contract drafting and review
  • Letters of demand
  • Property history research
  • Basic legal guidance

Mfanelo Advance Chauke

Certified Paralegal & Legal Practitioner in Training

Mr Mfanelo Advance Chauke is the founder and director of Advanced Paralegal Consultants (Pty) Ltd. He is a certified paralegal committed to providing reliable, confidential, and professional legal support to law firms and members of the public in South Africa.


Mr Chauke obtained his Diploma in Legal Assistance from the Vaal University of Technology in 2024 and completed Practical Training for Paralegals through the South African Paralegal Practice Training Institute (SAPPTI) in 2025. He is currently pursuing his LLB Degree at the University of South Africa (UNISA).


Since 2023, Mr Chauke has gained hands-on experience in the legal field through holiday work programmes at reputable law firms, where he also served as a paralegal. His practice is guided by professionalism, integrity, confidentiality, and efficiency, with a strong passion for improving access to justice in South Africa
